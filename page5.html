Put it into Microwave:
Once the sandwich is fully assembled and seasoned to perfection, carefully transfer it onto a microwave-safe plate. Place the plate into the microwave oven and heat the sandwich on high for a brief period, typically around 30 seconds to 1 minute, depending on your microwave's power settings. Keep an eye on the sandwich to prevent overheating and ensure the cheese melts slightly and the bread becomes warm and toasty.
